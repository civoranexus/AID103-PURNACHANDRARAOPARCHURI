üìå Getting Started with CropGuard AI

Welcome! This guide will help you set up your account and start using CropGuard AI to optimize your farm management.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address and create a strong password. You can also link your social media accounts for quick login.

2

Add Your Farms

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Farms and add all your farms. Provide farm location, size, and primary crops for accurate recommendations.

3

Set Your Preferences

Configure notification preferences, language, and units (metric/imperial). This helps personalize your experience.

4

Upload Your First Photo

Go to Photo Capture module and take a crop photo. Our AI will analyze the image for diseases and provide recommendations.

5

Monitor Your Dashboard

Check your dashboard daily for real-time farm health updates, weather forecasts, and AI recommendations.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is the disease detection? ‚ñº
Our AI model has 94.2% accuracy on common diseases like powdery mildew, early blight, and leaf rust. Accuracy improves with multiple photos from different angles. For best results, ensure good lighting and clear shots of affected areas.
Does CropGuard work offline? ‚ñº
The app requires internet for photo analysis and real-time updates. However, your dashboard data is cached and available offline. Disease detection, weather, and market prices need internet connectivity.
How much water can I save with irrigation optimization? ‚ñº
On average, farmers save 20-35% water while maintaining or increasing yields. Savings depend on your current practices, soil type, and crop. Real-time soil moisture monitoring is key to optimization.
Is my farm data private and secure? ‚ñº
Yes! Your data is encrypted with SSL/TLS and stored securely on our servers. You have full control over data sharing preferences. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What crops does CropGuard support? ‚ñº
